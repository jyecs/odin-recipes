Skillet Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Poweder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Chicken
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Boil water over high heat and cook spaghetti
  2. Heat oil in skillet and cook tomatoes and season
  3. Heat oil and cook chicken, mushrooms,and onions
  4. Mix ingredients in skillet
